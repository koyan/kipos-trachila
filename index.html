About us

Make yourself at home and enjoy a unique experience

Kipaki (pronounced 'keepAkee') in Greek means 'small garden'. It describes the house as it has a small garden, a green oasis in a small beautiful village.

Mani is a wild, rugged region that offers the most authentic holiday experience. Staying at Kipaki in Trachila is a feast for your eyes and medicine for your soul. Kipaki combines tradition with a modern style, magnificent views with comfort, easy access to the sea with a chance for small excursions and hikes.

Local Activities

Small Excursions and Hikes

Very close to Trahila you can find the pictoresque village of Ag. Nikolaos, the amazing beach of Kalogria in Stoupa and the most beautiful town of Kardamili, to go on one-day excursions. You can also rent bikes, a boat, or go on a hike.

Local Activities

Taste the food

Everyone knows how tasty the food in Greece is. Don't miss on a chance to try the local cuisine. You can find places to experience traditional food with a modern twist or of course Greece's best known street food, the Souvlaki.
But if the local tavern in Trahila is open, make absolutely sure you try the food there at least once.
